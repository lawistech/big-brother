Our Story

Family Owned & Operated

For nearly 5 years, Big Brother Moving has been serving customers in and around the Cary, Raleigh-Triangle, North Carolina. Our goal is to make a potentially stressful situation a comfortable, stress-free experience.

Big Brother Moving is fully licensed and insured, and focused on your comfort and trust. Most moves can be full of anxiety, but our experience will give you peace of mind, evident by the measures we take to care for your valuables.

Our philosophy is to only hire individuals who will pay attention to the importance of customer service. You will see the "Big Brother" difference when you work with our team to care for your belongings. We treat your property like it is our very own.
